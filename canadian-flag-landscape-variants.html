Canadian Flag — Landscape Orientation Variants

Using the official Wikipedia flag (proper 11-point leaf, accurate geometry). Shown at actual cover size with leather frame. Seven different ways a landscape flag can live on a portrait cover — with and without rotation, centered or as a band, with surrounding leather.

Band across the cover

1. Full width band flag spans full cover width at natural 2:1, leather visible top and bottom
2. Oversized band flag extends beyond panel edges, most visible area is flag
3. Flag banner, space below like a stamp near the top, leather plaque space below for logo/name

Rotated 90° (vertical flag)

4. Rotated 90° full flag on its side, red bars now top/bottom, leaf on its side
5. Rotated 90° with margin same rotation but sized down, leather border visible

Full cover — stretched to fit

6. Stretched to cover flag squashed into portrait — distorts proportions, leaf narrower
7. Framed band flag sits in its own sub-frame mid-cover, leather panels above and below with stitching

All variants use the official Wikipedia Canada flag SVG. Where the flag doesn't fill the whole cover, the remaining space would hold the course emblem badge, date plate, and StrokeMaster tag. Variant 1 and 3 leave the clearest room for those elements.
